Where to Find Guns and Ammo

The police station is the best place to find guns, ammo and body armor. Below are images of what you can expect to find

These items will be located in the lowest level of the police station. Always be extra careful when going to the police stations though. They are a very good place to encounter other players.

Next is the apartment complex

There apartment comlpex always spawns shotguns at the top of the stairwell. They're also a good place for food or clothes which can be shreded into scraps of clothe.

The apartment complex is realatively safe for the most part but you should always be cautious when going into any city.

And last but not least is the office building

The office building is by far the best place to find loads of pistol ammo.

Make sure you check every desk and all cabinets. You'll mostly find .44, .45, and 9mm ammo here. Agian these are highly populated areas so watch your back at all times.
